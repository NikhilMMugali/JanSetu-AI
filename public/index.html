The Process

How JanSetu AI Works

From spotting an issue to getting it resolved — a seamless, AI-driven pipeline that puts citizens first.

1

📱 Spot & Report

Snap a photo of any civic issue — pothole, broken light, garbage pile. Upload it through the citizen portal with one tap.

2

🤖 AI Analysis

Our AI instantly classifies the issue type, severity, and routes it to the right department. If AI is unsure, it goes to a city official for manual review.

3

🗳️ Community Voting

Citizens vote on reported issues. More votes = higher priority. Your community decides what matters most.

4

⚡ Smart Priority Engine

Our scoring engine combines AI severity, community votes, location impact, and recency to create a dynamic priority score.

5

🏛️ Municipal Action

Top-priority issues are pushed to municipal teams. Real-time tracking from assignment to resolution. Every step is transparent.

6

🏆 Points & Recognition

Earn points for reporting, voting, and validating. Climb the leaderboard. Your civic engagement gets recognized.
